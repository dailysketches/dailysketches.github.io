1. Draw a flat color
  2. Draw a partially transparent texture over the color
  3. Transform the texture, optionally without clearing the buffer

Note that if you don't clear the buffer, over time the texture may completely overwrite the flat color.

As the examples show, one option is to periodically clear the entire buffer. Another is to use a mask to delineate areas in your texture that will always be transparent.

void ofApp::setup(){
    increment = 0.02;
    plane.setup("partially-transparent.png");
}

void ofApp::update(){
    plane.incrementTextureScale(-increment);
}
