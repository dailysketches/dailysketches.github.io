Steve Roggenbuck: 'make something beautiful...'

  • permalink
  • show descripton

When I started watching this video my first impression was that it was annoying and I wasn't going to like it.

I kept watching anyway, and three minutes later was surprised to discover it was actually irritatingly inspiring. It's a testament to the power of music in video.

You could try parodying it by playing Sigur Ros over Paul Whitehouse's 'Brilliant!' sketches.

But it wouldn't work. It's more than that. The video is littered with attempts at self-awareness, deprecation and authenticity. I couldn't help but try another one.
